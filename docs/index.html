Thank you for using Oraclize. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Information We May Collect

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers
  • Location Information: Only with your explicit consent, for location-based services
  • Motion Data: For interactive features such as shaking for fortune sticks
  • Usage Data: App usage frequency, feature preferences, history
  • Purchase Records: Subscription status and purchase history

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your real name (unless voluntarily provided)
  • Contact information
  • Financial information (handled by Apple)

2. Use of Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Generate personalized readings
  • Save your history
  • Process subscriptions and restore purchases
  • Analyze usage to optimize experience
  • Send service-related notifications

3. AI Services and Data Processing

This app uses third-party AI services to generate readings.

Data Sent to AI Services

  • Your questions or keywords
  • Selected type and related parameters
  • Randomly generated elements (cards, lots, etc.)

Important: We do NOT send your personal identifying information to AI services.

4. Data Storage

Local Storage

  • History is stored locally on your device
  • Subscription status syncs via iCloud Keychain

We do not store your personal data on remote servers.

5. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted network transmission (HTTPS/TLS 1.3)
  • Secure local data storage
  • Regular security reviews

6. Third-Party Services

This app may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store (app distribution and subscription processing)
  • AI content generation services

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View history stored on your device
  • Delete data: Clear history within the app
  • Withdraw consent: Disable location or motion permissions in device settings
  • Cancel subscription: Manage subscriptions through App Store

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not specifically designed for children under 13. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
